## Angle of repose

Related guides: [Basic usage](../manual/basic-usage.md), [Python API overview](../python-api/index.md).

The angle of repose is the steepest angle at which a granular material can be piled without slumping. This example demonstrates how to simulate the formation of a granular pile by raining particles onto a horizontal plane and measure the resulting slope angle.
