\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class used to evaluate the unbalanced force ratio of particles in a DEM simulation. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classnetdem_1_1_unbalanced_force_ratio_evaluator}{Unbalanced\+Force\+Ratio\+Evaluator}} class calculates the unbalanced force ratio for all particles in the simulation.
